About Me

Hello again! My name is Mayukh Mallick. I live in Kolkata and work at Chaos Native as a Product designer & Engineer on LitmusChaos. Before this, I designed for Kubera Core, Chaos and Subscription in Mayadata Inc.

As a kid, I loved judging the usability of any product. My love for technolgy and electrical gadgets made me to try to rethink if I am seeing a problem in using a product can I make it better in using. Now, I often find myself writing documents for my new designs and penning down the solutions I created while designing the interface.

Besides work, I appreciate dabbling with gadgets, reading crime thrillers and playing Rainbow 6 Siege. Like every other zillennial, I like getting all my news as short structure stories from Twitter, Medium, Facebook and Youtube.
